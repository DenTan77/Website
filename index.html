About Me

I’m a final-year Physics Engineering student at Telkom University with a passion for material computation, instrumentation, and high performance computing (HPC). My research focuses on the electronic and optical properties of 2D materials using first-principles calculations (DFT, GW, and BSE) with tools such as Quantum ESPRESSO and Yambo. I am particularly interested in excitonic effects and quasiparticle band structures. In addition, I apply machine learning techniques to analyze and predict material properties, integrating data-driven approaches with physics-based simulations. Beyond computational research, I also develop IoT based monitoring systems, combining sensors, and web technologies to build practical instrumentation solutions.
